🤝 The Athlete’s Oath

You are down to clown and play games! Yes, there will be (some!) drinking — you don’t need to drink for every game, but expect at least an N/A pour here and there to keep things fair.

The games are a full ~8 hours of adult field day meets beer Olympics — so once you’re in, lock in!

But it’s not all competition — there’s a pool, hot tub, putt-putt course, sport court, the beach, and plenty more to kick back with whenever you need a breather.

If you’re not looking to compete, I’d still love for you to come — but please let me know ahead of time for team delegation!
